Interview with the sponsors
1/23/04 9:30 to 10 AM Meeting with Cheryl at Capital Center and conference call with Abbie.
  What is the current evaluation process?
    Janelle (Kalamath Falls) determines which classes need evaluation each term and sends the questionare (blue sheet) and the scantron (white sheet) to the appropriate campuses in the appropriate quantities. The campuses solicit volunteers through the admins (Cheryl for Capital) and each course gets a proctor. The evaluations are handed back to the office and seperated. The scantrons get sent to Kalamath Falls for processing and the questionares stay at the campuses. While the scantrons are being processed, the admins sort the questionares by course and type in the comments in a word document, print it out, and place it in the instructor's file. The scantron results are returned, grouped by instructor, in the form of a numeric matrix of question results.
