Actor:  Student

 

Description:  Usually a primary actor with the OwlEval. Individual(s) actively enrolled at Oregon Institute of Technology, OIT. Student initiates one to many cases of using the OwlEval system. The system will provide the Student with a means to submit their course evaluation on any courses that the student is currently enrolled. Student is identified only by access key.

 

 

Actor: Staffing (member of)

 

Description: An Employee acting as a secondary Actor to OwlEval facilitating in completion of the Use Case by a primary actor. OwlEval allows the Staffing the ability to easily edit, compile, and store information provided by the primary actor (Student).

 

 

Actor:  Administration

 

Description: An Employee who is usually a department manager or department Dean initiating response of the system in generating reports on evaluation information. OwlEval will provide a manager with the most recently updated course evaluation information.

 

 

Actor:  Faculty

 

Description:  An Employee who is usually full-time or adjunct instructor. Initiating response of the system in accessing reports on evaluation information. OwlEval will provide faculty with the most recently updated and compiled student evaluations.

 

 

Actor:    Database – DBMS

 

Description:  Eternal system providing current registration information for primary actor

 

 

Actor:     Time

 

Description:  Initiates temporal Use Cases such as course specific, quarterly (term) and yearly reports.
