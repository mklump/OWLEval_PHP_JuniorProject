•Considerations
–UI was developed with risk of DB corruption in mind.
–Do not give the user access to things that will break the program, we can handle that on our own.
–Accomplished by having defaults and giving options rather than blank fields
–Important for grouping results; they’re guaranteed to be the same.
–Must be appealing and intuitive; limited use of graphics and colors.
–only give the user buttons that they can actually use instead of choices they don’t need.
•Despite the ability to collect data, this may still go through several revisions before Beta.
–Subjectivity of style demands flexibilty.
